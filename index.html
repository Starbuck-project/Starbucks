About Us

Starbucks Coffee Company
Our Story
Founded in 1971, Starbucks Coffee Company has grown from a single store in Seattle's Pike Place Market to a globally recognized brand, synonymous with exceptional coffee and a distinctive customer experience. From our humble beginnings, our mission has been clear: to inspire and nurture the human spirit ‚Äì one person, one cup, and one neighborhood at a time.
Our Coffee
At Starbucks, we are passionate about ethically sourcing and roasting the highest-quality Arabica coffee. Our coffee buyers personally travel to coffee farms in Latin America, Africa, and Asia to select the finest beans. We are committed to ensuring our coffee is grown under the highest standards of quality, using sustainable and ethical practices. Our comprehensive approach, known as Coffee and Farmer Equity (C.A.F.E.) Practices, ensures economic transparency, social responsibility, and environmental leadership.
The Starbucks Experience
We believe that a great cup of coffee is best enjoyed in a welcoming and inclusive environment. Our stores are designed to be a haven, a place between home and work where you can relax, meet friends, or simply enjoy a moment of solitude. From our friendly baristas who craft each beverage with care, to our comfortable seating and thoughtfully designed spaces, every detail is curated to enhance your coffee experience.
Our People Our partners (employees) are the heart and soul of the Starbucks Experience. We are dedicated to creating a culture of warmth and belonging, where everyone is welcome. Our partners are empowered to deliver personalized service and foster meaningful connections with our customers and communities. Through our comprehensive training and development programs, we ensure that our partners have the tools they need to succeed and grow within the company.
Commitment to Sustainability
Sustainability is integral to our business. We strive to minimize our environmental footprint through recycling, energy conservation, and sustainable building practices. Our goal is to make every aspect of our business as green as possible, from the sourcing of our coffee beans to the design of our stores. We are also committed to reducing waste through our cup recycling initiatives and encouraging the use of reusable cups.
Join Us
At Starbucks, we invite you to join us in celebrating the rich tradition of coffee. Whether you are a coffee connoisseur or just beginning your journey, we welcome you to experience the warmth, quality, and community that define Starbucks. Together, let's inspire and nurture the human spirit, one cup at a time.
